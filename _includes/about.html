ChemSymphoria is an in-house symposium of the Department of Chemistry at IISER Pune. The symposium aims to celebrate Chemistry Day within the department and features presentations by distinguished scientists focusing on various aspects of innovative research in science and technology. The platform also hosts presentations by final-year Ph.D. students, postdocs, industry and academia alumni. Active involvement from students and faculty has been an integral part of ChemSymphoria over the years.
